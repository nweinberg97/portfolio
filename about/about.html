About Me

Hi, I'm Nitai! I'm a guy with many hobbies. I like writing, making music, surfing, traveling, hiking, playing and watching sports, among lot's of other things. But, most of all, I'm passionate about bringing people together through technology, building healthier communities, and creating a positive impact in the world. I believe that how we design physical and digital spaces can have a profound impact on our health and sense of connection with the world around us.

My 'why' comes from a deeply personal place. As a stroke survivor, I've faced challenges that shaped my outlook on life and my desire to help others find strength and community. This journey drives my work, from writing my book, 'Changing Tides', to building 'Search for the Good,' a platform that connects purpose-driven people and projects. I believe that personal stories and doing good have the power to inspire and connect us all.

Looking ahead, I hope to continue creating spaces—both physical and digital—that bring people together for the greater good. Whether it's through community-centered cafes, innovative tech solutions, or platforms that amplify positive stories, my goal is to build a world where connection and well-being are accessible to everyone. I'm excited about the future and the chance to work with others who share a vision for a healthier, more connected world.
