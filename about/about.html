About Me

Hi, I'm Nitai! My mission is to bring people together through technology, build healthier communities, and create a positive impact in the world. I'm passionate about the intersection of health, tech, and community, and I believe that thoughtfully designed spaces and digital tools can shape better habits and stronger connections. Whether it's through innovative apps, community-driven cafes, or storytelling, I aim to create environments where people can thrive.

My 'why' comes from a deeply personal place. As a stroke survivor, I've faced challenges that shaped my outlook on life and my desire to help others find strength and community. This journey drives my work, from writing my book, *Changing Tides*, to building 'Search for the Good,' a platform that connects purpose-driven people and projects. I believe that every story of resilience has the power to inspire and connect us all.

Looking ahead, I hope to continue creating spaces—both physical and digital—that bring people together for the greater good. Whether it's through community-centered cafes, innovative tech solutions, or platforms that amplify positive stories, my goal is to build a world where connection and well-being are accessible to everyone. I'm excited about the future and the chance to work with others who share a vision for a healthier, more connected world.
