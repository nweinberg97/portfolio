Trybe Social - Find Your People

Project Overview

Trybe Social is a platform designed to help individuals create close-knit groups of friends and connect with like-minded people...

Problem Identification

In today’s digital age, young adults often find it challenging to build close, authentic friendships...

Research and Ideation

The team explored social networking trends and conducted interviews with young adults...

Design Approach

  • Intentional Matching: Trybe Social uses an algorithm to connect users...
  • Community Building: Users can join or create “Trybes”...
  • Engagement Tools: Group chats for planning meetups and sharing experiences...
  • Event Planning: Users can plan private and public events within Trybes...

Implementation and Launch

The team built prototypes using Flutterflow, and I helped launch Trybe Social on the US App Store...

Current Status

The team continues to work on the project and implement new features on the App Store...

Taskly - Minimalistic Task Management Tool

Project Overview

Taskly is a live task management tool designed with simplicity and minimalism in mind. Users can organize and edit tasks without needing to create an account...

Purpose and Design Philosophy

Taskly focuses on reducing distractions with a minimalistic interface, allowing users to concentrate solely on their tasks...

Key Features

  • Minimalistic UI: A clean, user-friendly layout inspired by popular interfaces...
  • Playful Task Management: Users can delete tasks by dragging them into a garbage can and move tasks across various containers...
  • Account-Free Management: Taskly uses local storage, allowing users to manage tasks directly...

Search for the Good - Impactful Connections Platform

Project Overview

Search for the Good is a platform under development that connects individuals, projects, and brands dedicated to making a positive impact in the world...

Purpose and Solution

The platform seeks to counteract the negative aspects of traditional social media by providing a space where people can connect over shared values...

Current Progress

I am actively building the platform and have started engaging with potential users...

How It Works

  • Get an Interview: Prospective members undergo an interview to confirm their commitment to positive impact...
  • Create a Profile: Members share their values and intentions...
  • Use the Platform: Members can connect with others, explore projects, and discover brands aligned with their values...

Vision and Long-Term Goals

The ultimate goal is to become a global hub for impact-driven connections and collaboration...

Knit - Community-Building Platform

Project Overview

Knit is a community-building platform aimed at enhancing the quality of life for residents in long-term care facilities...

Purpose and Solution

Knit addresses the issue of social isolation among older adults, especially those in long-term care homes...

Key Features and Strategy

  • Subscription Model: Knit generates revenue through facility and provider subscriptions...
  • Targeted Approach: Focused on long-term care homes in Vancouver...
  • Collaborative Model: Knit partners with local businesses to offer engaging activities...

Market and Impact

Knit taps into a market of older adults living in long-term care facilities in Vancouver, creating vibrant, supportive communities...
