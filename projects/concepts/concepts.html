Circles

Project Overview

Circles is a platform that creates micro-communities of 5-7 individuals who collaborate to reach shared health goals. By providing structured meeting formats and resources, Circles fosters support and accountability, helping users build sustainable habits.

Circles Slide Deck

Problem Identification

Many individuals lack a supportive, structured environment to pursue their health and wellness goals. Existing platforms are often too broad or impersonal, which limits effective accountability and motivation for lasting change.

Research and Ideation

Through research, I found a need for a platform that supports small, committed groups. Insights from user interviews revealed that people sought accountability partners and intimate groups to help them stay motivated. This led to the creation of Circles, where users could find like-minded individuals and foster meaningful connections.

Design Approach

  • Group Formation: Users form circles based on goals and availability, creating small, dedicated communities.
  • Meeting Structures: Customizable templates include goal-setting, check-ins, and structured activities to maintain focus.
  • Persona-Driven Features: Features like scheduling, goal assistance, and curated resources ensure relevance to each user’s journey.
  • Leader Support: Tools for leaders, including 24/7 support, coaching, and expert content, ensure effective facilitation.

Implementation and Prototyping

After creating wireframes, I tested prototypes with users and iterated based on feedback. Key features included a seamless onboarding experience, easy meeting setup, and personalized recommendations to boost engagement.

Circles Wireframes

Results and Reflections

Early users reported increased accountability and motivation, as well as reduced isolation. The design allowed for scalability and flexibility, adaptable to various wellness themes. Through Circles, I demonstrated my ability to create user-centered, impactful solutions that integrate digital tools with human connection.

Memo

Project Overview

MEMO is a fitness discovery platform designed to help users find creative ways to stay active and healthy. For users, MEMO offers personalized fitness challenges, content recommendations, and access to innovative products and services that connect them with online communities and thoughtful brands. For fitness studios, MEMO provides a tool to distribute content, engage users, and adapt to the digital fitness landscape, allowing them to reach new audiences and showcase their offerings effectively.

Memo Slide Deck

Problem Identification

During the COVID-19 pandemic, as digital fitness became increasingly prevalent, users were often overwhelmed by options and lacked inspiration for maintaining an active lifestyle. Fitness brands also faced challenges in engaging users and staying relevant in the rapidly shifting digital space. MEMO bridges this gap by offering a curated platform for fitness exploration, while supporting brands in adapting to these changes.

Research and Ideation

To shape MEMO’s features and focus, interviews were conducted with four fitness studios. These discussions highlighted the need for a platform that could personalize fitness experiences and facilitate connections between users and innovative fitness brands. Based on this feedback, MEMO was developed as a content discovery platform that supports both users and fitness studios.

Design Approach

  • Fitness Challenges and Personalized Content: MEMO provides users with a variety of fitness challenges and a content feed tailored to their interests, fostering motivation and commitment.
  • Exploration of Brands and Services: The platform allows users to discover brands and services that align with their wellness goals, enhancing their engagement with the fitness community.
  • Support for Fitness Studios: MEMO helps fitness studios adapt to digital trends by offering a space where they can showcase unique content and connect with potential clients.

Prototype Development

The prototype demonstrates MEMO’s core functionality, allowing users to sign up, personalize their fitness interests, and browse curated content feeds. For fitness studios, the prototype offers tools to create accounts, organize content, and distribute it effectively, showcasing trending and revenue-generating options to maximize engagement.

Tender

Tender - A Dating App for Foodies

Tender is an app concept that reinvents dating by connecting users over a shared love of food. Men showcase their cooking skills through videos, which women rate to create matches based on mutual food interests. The app encourages users to discover new cuisines and develop cooking skills, fostering a unique food-centric dating experience. Monetization would involve partnerships with local restaurants and a premium tier offering meal prep services and cooking tutorials.
